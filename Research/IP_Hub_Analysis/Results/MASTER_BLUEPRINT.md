# NovaDraft: Master Business Blueprint

## 1. Executive Summary
**NovaDraft** is an IP Development Hub tailored for independent inventors. It solves the massive gap between DIY (which suffers an 85% USPTO rejection rate due to formatting and obviousness issues) and hiring a patent lawyer (averaging $12,000+). 

The platform offers AI-driven brainstorming, claim augmentation, and strict formatting templates. **Crucially, NovaDraft does not submit patents on behalf of the user, avoiding unauthorized practice of law.** Instead, it generates perfectly formatted outputs that users can simply "copy and paste" into the official USPTO submission forms.
